A decent cup in four steps

01

Weigh

18 g of coffee for 300 g of water. Buy a scale before a fancy grinder.

02

Grind

Medium, like coarse sand. Grind right before you brew, never the night before.

03

Bloom

Pour 50 g of 94°C water, wait 40 seconds, watch it puff up.

04

Finish

Pour the rest in slow circles. Aim for a total brew around three minutes.
